About Us

The Gold Standard in Vedic Education Since 1998

Our Legacy

More Than Just an Institute

Parashari Institute stands as India's leading educational institution in Vedic sciences. Founded in 1998, we have submitted ourselves to the preservation of the sacred oral traditions of Vedic Astrology, Palmistry, and Vastu Shastra.

Named after the Maharishi Parashara, the father of Vedic Astrology, we blend ancient sutras with modern analytical techniques to create practitioners who are both intuitive and scientific.

Our Vision: To empower a new generation of ethical astrologers who guide society with wisdom, empathy, and precision.
