Understanding Vastu

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science that governs the spatial arrangement and orientation of structures. It is based on the belief that the environment in which we live has a profound effect on our well-being, health, prosperity, and happiness.

Vastu principles combine architectural wisdom, directional science, and the five elements to create spaces that promote positive energy flow.

  • ✓ Directional principles (5 elements)
  • ✓ Space optimization techniques
  • ✓ Color and material applications
  • ✓ Harmonious living environments
