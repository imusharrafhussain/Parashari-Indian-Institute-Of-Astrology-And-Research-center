Does the length of my Life Line determine how long I will live? +
No — this is the biggest myth in palmistry. The Life Line indicates vitality, energy levels, and major life changes — not the duration of life. This course will teach you to read all lines with correct interpretations, free from common misconceptions.
Should I read the left hand or the right hand? +
Both. Generally, the non-dominant hand reveals your innate potential and past influences, while the dominant hand shows what you have actively developed. This course teaches you to compare both for a complete reading.
Can palm lines change over time? +
Yes. Palm lines do evolve with age, experience, and significant life events. This is powerful evidence that palmistry reflects your living energy — and this course teaches you to track these changes for predictive purposes.
Can I predict career success or financial outcomes from the palm? +
Absolutely. The Fate Line, Sun Line, and Mercury Line — combined with mount analysis — reveal career direction, recognition potential, and financial aptitude. You will learn to read all of these with precision.
