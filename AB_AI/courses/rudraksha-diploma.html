How can I tell if a Rudraksha bead is genuine or fake? +
This course teaches scientific authentication methods — water float tests, X-ray analysis, visual inspection of mukhi lines, and texture examination — so you can confidently identify genuine Rudraksha from imitations.
Can wearing the wrong Rudraksha cause negative effects? +
While Rudraksha is generally considered safe, wearing a mukhi that amplifies an already strong planet can create imbalance. This is why chart-based prescription — which you will learn here — is crucial before recommending any bead.
Is there a specific ritual required before wearing Rudraksha? +
Yes. Shuddhi (purification) and Pran Pratishtha (energization) rituals activate the bead's spiritual power. This course teaches you the complete Vedic process, including the specific mantras for each mukhi type.
Can Rudraksha therapy help with health issues like blood pressure or anxiety? +
Research and traditional texts suggest specific mukhis have therapeutic effects — for example, 5-mukhi for blood pressure and 2-mukhi for emotional balance. This course covers the therapeutic applications backed by both scripture and modern observations.
