Can I really assess someone's character just by looking at their face? +
Yes. Physiognomy maps specific facial features — forehead shape, eye spacing, nose type, chin structure — to personality traits and life tendencies. With practice, you can read character, health, and potential with surprising accuracy.
Is Face Reading useful in professional settings like job interviews? +
Absolutely. Face Reading is increasingly used in HR, sales, and leadership assessment. You will learn to identify traits like ambition, trustworthiness, and decision-making style from facial features alone.
Do facial features change over time, and does that affect readings? +
Yes, faces change with age and experience. This course teaches you to read aging patterns, wrinkle formations, and facial color changes as indicators of life-phase transitions and health shifts.
Can I combine Face Reading with Palmistry or Astrology? +
Yes. Multi-system integration — combining face reading with palm analysis and astrological data — creates a far more comprehensive personality and destiny profile. This course covers those cross-referencing techniques.
