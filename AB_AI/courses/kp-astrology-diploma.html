How is KP Astrology different from traditional Vedic Astrology? +
KP Astrology uses the Placidus house system and the revolutionary Sub-Lord Theory to provide binary YES/NO answers with pinpoint timing — a level of precision that traditional Parashari methods cannot match alone.
Can KP really give exact dates for events? +
Yes. By combining Sub-Lord analysis, Ruling Planets, and transit triggers, KP practitioners can narrow predictions down to specific months, weeks, and even days — which is its greatest strength.
Do I need to know Vedic Astrology before learning KP? +
No. While having a basic understanding of planets and houses helps, this course is structured to teach KP from scratch. Many students find KP easier to learn because of its rule-based, logical approach.
Can I use KP for Horary (Prashna) questions without a birth chart? +
Yes. KP Horary Astrology allows you to answer specific questions using just a number between 1–249 provided by the querent — no birth chart or birth time needed at all.
