Understanding Palmistry

Hasta Shastra or Palmistry is an ancient divination practice that dates back thousands of years. By analyzing the lines, mounds, and shapes on the human hand, a skilled palmist can reveal information about personality, health, relationships, career, and future events.

Each line and marking on the palm has a specific meaning and can provide profound insights into a person's life journey.
