What is Vedic Astrology?

Vedic Astrology, also known as Jyotish Shastra, is an ancient Indian science that explores the relationship between celestial bodies and human life. Based on the Vedas, it offers profound insights into personality, destiny, and life events.

Unlike Western astrology, Vedic Astrology uses the sidereal zodiac and focuses on lunar nodes (Rahu and Ketu), dasha systems, and detailed predictive techniques.

  • ✓ Ancient science dating back 5000+ years
  • ✓ Mathematical precision in calculations
  • ✓ Accurate life predictions and guidance
  • ✓ Spiritual and practical applications
